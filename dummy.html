Welcome To STACK-CUP™

Sustainable, Stackable, Reusable, Branded Cups

  • We are experts in launching and running reusable cup programmes for stadiums, festivals, and major events. Our flagship product, STACK-CUP™, removes the need for cup carriers and trays, decreasing queue times and increasing bar spend.

    We make reusable cup programmes simple for our clients and their customers. From hire and purchase models to cup returns options, let us help your customers to waste less, carry more!

Cups For Festivals And Events

We work with a number of festival organisers who come to us to support their sustainability ethos. The benefits of reusable cups at festivals and events are huge. We provide a cost-effective solution that stops event organisers from having to purchase disposable cups year on year.

Cups For Venues

We work with venues that are looking for an alternative to single use plastic. Our multi carry solution eradicates the need for cup carriers and trays which will reduce waste and venues increase bar sales by reducing wait and queue times.
